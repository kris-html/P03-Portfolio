Rivals

The purpose of this passion project was to depict the rivalry and opposition between two players in a tournament match for the first-person shooter game Overwatch 2 by means of an illustration. I was inspired by when after the match, when the broadcast for the tournament displayed an infographic between two players who were playing the same character, Sombra. In this illustration I needed to depict the identities of these players, and integrate their image with the design of Sombra, a technomacer with technology imbeded into her body capable of hacking with just her fingertips.

In my inital draft sketch I wanted to include as many aspects of the player's team and the character they play into the overall design. They are the star players for their respective team, so I opted to include their team logo in the bachground. It created a focus point where then I can lead the users eyes downwards by using a spotlight originating above them.

After I was satisfied with the line art, I started to render my illustration. I worked with one of the portraits first, as I felt jumping between the two could create inconsistencies and hinder my abilities. The colour palettes I selected are inspired on their team colours. In this case, the left portrait uses primarily green and black, while the right portrait uses mainly red and white.

As I approached the finish line for this piece, I decided to ultimately remove the other player half of this illustration. When viewing the piece as a whole, the two contrasting colours made more of a separation between the players than I intended for. It created the illustrations as individual portraits.

Reflection

I am quite happy with how this illustration turned out, despite it turning into a single portrait piece I learned a lot about composition and where to use color. I feel as I avoided overloading the viewer with bright colors, only using the green to highlight and create a glow effect around my subject.
