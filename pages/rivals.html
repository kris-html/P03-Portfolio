Rivals

The purpose of this passion project was to depict the rivalry and opposition between two players in a tournament match for the first-person shooter game Overwatch 2 by means of an illustration. I needed to express the identities of the players and integrate their image with the design of the character they both play.

In my inital draft sketch I wanted to include aspects of the player's team and the character they play into the overall design. It created a focus point where I can lead the users eyes downwards by using a spotlight originating above them.

I worked on one of the portraits first, as jumping between the two felt inconsistant and hindered my abilities. The colour palette I selected are inspired on their team colours in order to further connect them to their team.

I decided to ultimately remove the other player of this illustration. Viewing the piece as a whole created two contrasting colours that made more of a separation between the players than I wanted.

Reflection

I learned a lot about composition and where to selectively use color. I feel I was able to avoid overloading the viewer with bright colors, only using the green to highlight my subject.
