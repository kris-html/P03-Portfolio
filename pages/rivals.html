Rivals

The purpose of this passion project was to depict the rivalry and opposition between two players in a tournament match for the first-person shooter game Overwatch 2 by means of an illustration. I was inspired by when after the match, when the broadcast for the tournament displayed an infographic between two players who were playing the same character, Sombra. In this illustration I needed to depict the identities of these players, and integrate their image with the design of Sombra, a technomacer with technology imbeded into her body capable of hacking with just her fingertips.
