The Peak Cover

The Peak's production and design editor Josh Ralla reached out to me to create the week 3 cover illustration. This illustration is based off the featured article on the Gateway Theatre's six-episode audio play that delves into 'Chinese traditions and festivities while conjuring childhood experiences of Lunar New Year Celebrations'. Josh requested some elements which included: a red and gold dominant background with symbolic Lunar New Year objects scattered throughout and images of objects specifically mentioned in the play around the bottom.

This was the check-in piece I sent to Josh for review. I wanted to get his, and the production team's opinion on any changes or adjustments that could be made to my current illustration. They were satisfied with the product so far, so from here I proceeded with finalizing the illustration into it's final version and sent it off to the Josh.

Reflection

To be honest I was nervous creating this piece, this is a topic close to my culture and I wanted to get all the elements correct. Thinking back in hindsight I think I ended up overcrowding the overall composition with all the objects I wanted to include. There wasn't enough breathing space and use of negative space to further enhance the piece. But with how this was my first commission for a cover, I am very happy with how it turned out.
